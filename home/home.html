ABOUT

Welcome to Interstellar
A dynamic web application that allows users to explore the wonders of our solar system in 3D and explore the planet by ex.
Our mission is to educate and inspire individuals about space, astronomy, and the celestial bodies that inhabit our universe.

We have also developed a virtual world Unreal Engine to make exploring the planets even more immersive and realistic. With this cutting-edge technology, users can experience a lifelike journey through space, exploring planets, asteroids, and comets as if they were truly there.

With an interactive orrery at the core of our platform, users can navigate through planets, asteroids, and comets, gaining insights into their characteristics and behaviors. Join us in our quest to explore the cosmos!

How To Use

To explore the solar system in 3D, click on the "Explore" button. This will take you to a 3D visualization of the solar system. You can hover over the Sun and planets to view information about them. For more detailed and accurate information, click on the dropdown menu and select a planet. A new page will appear with comprehensive details about that planet.
If you'd like to test your knowledge, click on the "Exam" button. To dive into the virtual world of any planet, click on the "VR Planet" button for an immersive experience.
